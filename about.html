About Us

Welcome to Delaram's Restaurant! We are a family-owned business that has been serving delicious kosher meals for over 20 years. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction is what sets us apart. We take pride in using only the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes to create mouthwatering dishes that our customers love.

Our team of talented chefs and friendly staff are dedicated to providing you with an exceptional dining experience. Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch or a leisurely dinner, we strive to make every visit memorable. Thank you for choosing Delaram's Restaurant, and we look forward to serving you soon!

Our Mission

At Delaram's Restaurant, our mission is to provide the highest quality kosher dining experience while maintaining the warmth and hospitality that our customers have come to expect. We are committed to sourcing the finest ingredients and preparing each dish with care and attention to detail.

We believe that food is not just about nourishment, but also about bringing people together. Our goal is to create a welcoming environment where friends and family can gather to enjoy delicious meals and create lasting memories. We are dedicated to upholding the traditions of kosher cuisine while also embracing innovation and creativity in our menu offerings.
